Garlic Parm Pasta

A simple tasty pasta dish, quick and easy. Save on the clean up and make everything in the same pot!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk, or more, as needed
  • 8 ounces uncooked fettuccine
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  2. Add garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Stir in chicken broth, milk and fettuccine; season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  4. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until pasta is cooked through, about 18-20 minutes.
  5. Stir in Parmesan. If the mixture is too thick, add more milk as needed until desired consistency is reached.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with parsley, if desired.
