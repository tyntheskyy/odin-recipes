Sweet Potato Pie

A delicious dessert, my family recipe is a secret (and I forgot it) so we'll be working with something different. Hopefully it's as good as I know it can be, good luck fellow experimenters.

Ingredients

  • Dough for single-crust pie
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes (about 1-1/2 pounds), peeled and cubed
  • 1/3 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature, lightly beaten
  • 3/4 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°. On a lightly floured surface, roll dough to a 1/8-in.-thick circle; transfer to a 9-in. pie plate.
  2. Trim crust to 1/2 in. beyond rim of plate; flute edge. Refrigerate while preparing filling.
  3. Place sweet potatoes in a medium saucepan; add water to cover. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat; cook, uncovered, until tender, 13-15 minutes. Drain potatoes; return to pan. Mash until very smooth; cool to room temperature.
  5. In a bowl, cream butter and sugar. Add eggs; mix well. Add milk, 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt; mix well. Pour into crust.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes.
  7. Reduce heat to 350°; bake until set or a knife inserted in the center comes out clean, 35-40 minutes.
  8. Cool on a wire rack. Refrigerate leftovers.
